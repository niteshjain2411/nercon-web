Reaching AIIMS Guwahati

Address
All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Changsari, Kamrup, Assam 781101, India
  • From Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi Airport: AIIMS Guwahati is approximately 27 km away and can be reached by taxi or app-based cab services.
  • From Kamakhya Railway Station: The campus is around 18 km away, making it a convenient drive by cab or local transport.
  • By road: The institute is located at Changsari and is accessible from Guwahati city through NH routes with direct taxi connectivity.

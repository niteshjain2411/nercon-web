About the Conference

The Department of Pathology and Lab Medicine at AIIMS Guwahati cordially invites postgraduate students, delegates, clinicians, and medical researchers to the 35th Annual Conference of the North Eastern Regional Chapter, Indian Association of Pathologists and Microbiologists.

This premier event brings together pathologists, diagnosticians, and healthcare professionals from across the Northeast region of India and across the globe.

Guwahati, situated on the banks of the majestic Brahmaputra River, offers the perfect backdrop for this prestigious conference. The city is known for its natural beauty, welcoming hospitality, and growing medical infrastructure.

NERCON 2026 is designed to enhance your knowledge and keep you updated with the latest advances in pathology and laboratory medicine. The conference will feature keynote lectures from renowned experts, interactive workshops, panel discussions, career guidance sessions and cutting-edge research presentations.

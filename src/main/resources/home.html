About the Conference

The Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at AIIMS Guwahati cordially invites delegates, practitioners, and specialists to the 35th Annual Conference of NERCON 2026. This premier event brings together pathologists, microbiologists, and healthcare professionals from across the Northeast region.

Guwahati, situated on the banks of the majestic Brahmaputra River, offers the perfect backdrop for this prestigious conference. The city is known for its natural beauty, welcoming hospitality, and growing medical infrastructure.

NERCON 2026 is designed to enhance your knowledge and keep you updated with the latest advances in pathology and microbiology. The conference will feature keynote lectures from renowned experts, interactive workshops, panel discussions, and cutting-edge research presentations.

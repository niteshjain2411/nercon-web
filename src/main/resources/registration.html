Terms & Conditions for Registration (Conference & Workshops)

  • Delegates registering under the Postgraduate (PG) category must upload a valid certificate/letter duly signed by the Head of Department at the time of registration.
  • Registered accompanying persons shall be granted access to the conference venue, including designated meal services and the banquet dinner.
  • Registration fees may be paid via UPI or NEFT transfer only. Delegates are advised to verify the payable amount prior to the transaction.
  • The Transaction ID and date must be recorded, and a screenshot of the payment confirmation should be retained until formal acknowledgment is received from the organizing committee.

Workshop Registration Policy

  • Delegates may register for:
    • One Full-Day Workshop, or
    • A maximum of two Half-Day Workshops, provided they are non-overlapping.
  • Concessional Registration (Half-Day Workshops):
    • Delegates opting for two non-overlapping half-day workshops simultaneously at the time of initial registration shall be eligible for a concession of ₹1,000.
    • This concession is applicable only at the time of combined registration for both workshops.
    • The concession shall not be applicable if a delegate registers for one half-day workshop initially and subsequently opts for another.

General Provisions

  • Registration shall be deemed complete only upon successful payment and verification by the organizing committee.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to review, accept, or decline any registration in cases of incomplete documentation or non-compliance with the stated guidelines.
  • Delegates are advised to adhere strictly to the above conditions to ensure a seamless registration experience.
  • For any queries or technical assistance regarding the registration process, delegates may contact: nercon2026@gmail.com

Cancellation Policy

  1. Cancellation requests, duly mailed to nercon2026@gmail.com, will be accepted till 30th September 2026. After this date, no request for cancellation will be accepted.
  2. Eligible cancellation requests will be eligible for 50% refund of the amount paid.
  3. Refund will be processed two weeks after the conference.
